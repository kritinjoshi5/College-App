SS College Of Engineering

SS College of Engineering (SSCE) is situated in lush hills of Aravali.Campus is well connected to main City Station ,Railway Station,AirPort and other facillities of the city.Campus Buildings have been designed and planned according to the norms and the environment here is completely eco-friendly.

History
SSCE was established under the aegis of SS Education Trust which was set up in the year 2007. The institute is affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University, Kota and recognised by AICTE, New Delhi.
Campus
The institute has following major facilities: Separate hostel facility for boys & girls State of the art laboratories Well stocked digital library with DELNET facility Internet Lab Seminar/Conference Hall Well equipped Lecture Theatre Sports & Recreational facility Transport facility Classrooms with audio-visual aids Medical facility Research Publication
